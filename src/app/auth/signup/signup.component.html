Interpolate is a platform for sharing annotations on PDF documents

Interpolate is much more than a platform for creating sticky-note-annotations and sharing it with other people. We designed Interpolate as a Q & A platform, where users can directly post questions inside the documents in the form of annotations. This means you can:

  • Reply to annotations that are shared with you;

  • Have a nested-discussion;

  • Easily navigate to an annotation through our search engine.

As we grow our platform, we will add more features based on users' feedback.

You will find Interpolate helpful, if you are a

  • Researcher

  • Instructor

  • Student
