Groups is an app that enable users in the same group to share annotations amongst each other.

If you are a student, you can use this app to
  • Create a study group
  • Add your friends to the study group
  • Upload and share lecture material in your study group
  • Post questions on the shared documents
  • Help each other by responding to questions
To create a group,
  • Click on "Create a group".
  • After filling out "group name" and "description", click "Create group"
You are able to invite other others to join your group by sending them a link (you can see more detail about this once you navigated to a particular group). You can see a list of groups that you are a member of at "My groups". To navigate to one of them, just click on the group name.

Classes is an app for instructors and student.

If you are an instructor, you can use this app to
  • Create a class
  • Invite your students to join your class
  • Upload and share lecture material to your students
  • Help your students by responding to their questions
  • Teach more efficiently
If you are a student, you can use this app to
  • Join a class
  • Post questions on the materials your instructor uploaded
  • Help each other by responding to other students' question
  • Learn better
To create a class,
  • Click on "Create a class".
  • After filling out "class name" and "description", click "Create class"
You are able to invite other others to join your class by sending them a link (you can see more detail about this once you navigated to a particular class). You can see a list of classes that you are a member of at "My classes". To navigate to one of them, just click on the class name.
